Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Business Workflow Mastery collects information people voluntarily submit through forms, scheduling links, messages, or other direct interactions. This may include name, email address, phone number, business information, and details about workflow or operational needs.

How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries.
  • To schedule and manage calls or consultations.
  • To provide information about Business Workflow Mastery services, resources, or community access.
  • To improve our internal follow-up and customer support process.

SMS and mobile information

If you provide your phone number and opt in to receive SMS messages, Business Workflow Mastery may send messages related to your inquiry, appointment scheduling, and business workflow consulting information.

Mobile information will not be sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Opting out

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. You may reply HELP for help. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

Information sharing

We may use service providers to operate forms, scheduling, messaging, analytics, or customer support systems. These providers are used only to support the business relationship and are not permitted to use mobile information for their own marketing purposes.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the contact method used to reach Business Workflow Mastery or through the original inquiry channel.
